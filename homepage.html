About me

Hi! My name is Jackson and I am a Boston-based musician and music teacher. Music has been an obsession of mine since childhood. I've been playing guitar since before I can remember and taking it seriously since I was about 13. Now I am sharing my expertise by teaching guitar and music theory to all levels and styles of musician.
